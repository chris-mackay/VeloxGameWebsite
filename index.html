Velox

Velox is a game for Windows (7, 8, & 10)



Velox_Setup-v1.0.exe


You have 30 lives to beat the game in one sitting. Think you can do it? Oh yeah, and each level is timed.. so.. there’s that.

Velox was designed to be played with a controller. Keyboard is not supported. XBOX 360 and XBOX One Controllers are where it’s at.
